Hobbies & Interests

💡 生活哲學

我相信生活的豐富度來自於多元的體驗與持續的探索。從底層韌體到產品策略，從創業實踐到 AI 技術前沿，20 年的專業淬鍊始終保持對創新的熱情與洞察力。工作之外，我在科技與自然之間找到平衡，透過藝術創作、生態觀察、空間設計與動手實作，讓技術思維與生活美學相互滋養、融會貫通。

🎨 藝術與創作

  • 從小熱愛繪畫，創作對我來說是一種療癒與自我對話的過程。不論是水彩、素描還是數位繪圖，透過色彩與線條，我學會了觀察細節、表達情感。這份美學敏感度，也自然延伸到我在產品設計上的視覺思維。

🌿 與自然共生

  • 飼養鳥類 - 每天觀察牠們的互動，是我最放鬆的時光。鳥兒讓我學會傾聽、耐心與尊重生命的獨特性格。
  • 飼養烏龜 - 牠們的慢節奏提醒我，不是所有事情都需要急著完成。慢，是一種哲學，也是一種長期陪伴的承諾。
  • 種植果樹 - 親手體驗「從種子到果實」的過程，就像打造一個產品從 MVP 到成熟，每一階段都蘊含等待與成長的價值。

🏗️ 空間與實作

  • 室內設計 - 我親自參與過多個空間設計案，從動線規劃、材質選擇到家具配置，轉化生活想像為實際空間的過程與產品設計非常相似。
  • DIY 實作 - 不只設計，我也熱愛動手做。配線、水管、木工、家具組裝，我享受從 0 到 1 的實作過程，這讓我在技術開發上也更能掌握細節與真實需求。

🚀 技術在生活中的實踐

  • IoT 物聯網 - 為寵物空間與果樹溫室自建監控與自動化系統，應用感測器、數據分析，實際改善居住與栽種環境。技術對我來說，不只是解決問題的工具，更是實現美好生活的途徑。
  • 無人機應用 - 透過 GPS 進行 3D 建模，結合空拍攝影技術，並應用於 UWB 定位系統的校準工作，實現精準定位技術的驗證與優化。

🏃‍♀️ 運動與生活

  • 慢跑 - 定期慢跑是我維持身心健康的方式。在跑步的過程中，我可以放空思緒、釋放壓力，有時候最好的靈感反而在奔跑中浮現。跑步教會我持之以恆與自我紀律的重要性。
  • 家人之間的協作 - 在我們家，家務不是分配給某一個人默默完成的工作，而是一場家人之間的協作。從洗衣、洗碗，到澆水整理陽台上的植栽，我們像小小的團隊一樣，一起討論、分工、互相幫忙。這些看似瑣碎的日常，其實是我們相處最密切的時光——孩子在其中學會支持彼此，也懂得為一個共享的環境付出心力。我一直相信，美好的生活環境，不只是靠流程或管理建立出來的，而是來自每一個參與者心裡那份愛與守候。

"這些日常，不只是興趣愛好，而是我對世界的理解方式。技術與藝術、理性與感性，在生活中並不衝突，而是可以交織出獨特節奏與風格。我享受在各種角色中切換，也樂於把這些經驗，轉化為可以創造價值的力量。"

— Emma Tan

💡 Philosophy of Life

I believe that a rich life comes from diverse experiences and ongoing exploration. From low-level firmware to product strategy, from startup practice to the frontier of AI, my 20 years of experience have always been grounded in a passion for innovation and a sharp eye for change. Outside of work, I find balance between technology and nature—through art, ecological observation, spatial design, and hands-on building. It's where technical thinking and aesthetic living meet and nourish each other.

🎨 Art & Creative Practice

  • I've loved drawing since childhood—using lines and colors to capture beautiful moments or express boundless imagination. Whether it's watercolor, sketching, or digital painting, the creative process is a form of healing and self-dialogue for me. Through it, I've learned to observe details and express emotions—skills that naturally extend into how I approach visual design in products.

🌿 Nature & Ecology

  • Bird keeping - I share my home with a few lively feathered friends. Observing their behavior and learning their habits is one of the most calming parts of my day. They've taught me patience, attentiveness, and how to appreciate the unique personality of each living being.
  • Turtle keeping - Turtles move slowly, and their pace has reminded me that not everything in life needs to be fast. Caring for them has been a lesson in the philosophy of slowness—of patience, quiet observation, and long-term companionship.
  • Fruit tree growing - From choosing seedlings and preparing soil to pruning and harvesting, growing fruit trees has let me experience the entire journey from seed to fruit. It's a process full of waiting, learning, and joy—very much like building a product from MVP to maturity.

🏗️ Space Design & Making

  • Interior design - I've designed several living spaces myself—from layout and style decisions to material selection and furniture placement. I enjoy turning abstract ideas of daily life into tangible, livable space. Design isn't just about aesthetics; it's also about flow, function, storage, and user experience—principles that overlap with product design.
  • DIY & hands-on making - With a background in firmware development, I'm not only a planner—I love getting my hands dirty. From wiring and plumbing to furniture building and small woodworking projects, I enjoy the challenge of making things from scratch. It's where I fully engage with problem-solving and the often-overlooked details that bring real understanding and satisfaction.

🚀 Tech Exploration in Daily Life

  • IoT (Internet of Things) - I actively explore IoT applications—from smart homes to industrial automation—and apply them in real life. I've built monitoring systems for my pets (temperature, humidity, air and water quality) and smart control systems for my fruit greenhouse (soil moisture, auto-irrigation, temperature, lighting). Technology, to me, isn't just for work—it's a way to improve the quality of everyday life.
  • Drone applications - Leveraging GPS for 3D modeling combined with aerial photography, and applying it to UWB positioning system calibration for validating and optimizing precision location technology.

🏃‍♀️ Sports & Lifestyle

  • Jogging - Regular jogging maintains my physical and mental health. While running, I can clear my mind and release stress—sometimes the best ideas emerge during a run. Running teaches me the importance of perseverance and self-discipline.
  • Collaboration Among Family - In our home, chores aren't assigned to one person to handle alone — they're a shared effort among all of us. From doing laundry and washing dishes to tending the plants on our balcony, we work together like a small team: planning, dividing tasks, and helping each other out. These everyday routines, simple as they may seem, are when we connect the most — where our children learn to support one another and understand what it means to care for a shared space. I truly believe that a beautiful home isn't built only through systems or management, but through the quiet love and care each person brings into it.

"These aren't just hobbies—they're how I engage with the world. Whether I'm painting, planting, designing a space, or wiring up a smart system, I see it all as part of the same mindset: a curiosity about how things work and a drive to make life more meaningful. For me, learning never stops—it simply takes new forms and new tools."

— Emma Tan
